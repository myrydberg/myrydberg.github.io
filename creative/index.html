Real-time animation in OpenGL and SGCT

The bechdel test

Visualising and analysing data

The purpose of the project was to visulise how films through the years have passed the bechdel test. The test serves to differenciate between films worthy of watching from films lacking good female representation.

We used two data sets, one containing the results from the bechdeltest and the other from IMDb with information about grossings, score and actors. The later was used to give more context to the results from the test.

The full application can be found at the Bechdel test.

  • Date: March 2017
  • Course: TNM048 - Information visualisation
  • Techniques used: html, javascript, D3.js
